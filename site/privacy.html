Privacy Policy

Short version: This extension stores your dictionary locally in your browser using Chrome storage. It does not transmit any data to external servers. No analytics, no tracking, no remote code.

What data is stored?

  • Your anonymization rules (patterns and replacements), and which sites you've enabled.

Where is it stored?

In Chrome's local extension storage on your device. If you export/import, the JSON file remains under your control.

What permissions are used?

  • storage to save your rules locally.
  • activeTab and scripting to inject the content script only on sites you enable.
  • optional_host_permissions to request access per-site when you enable it.
  • clipboardWrite to place sanitized text onto your clipboard.
  • contextMenus for right‑click options.
